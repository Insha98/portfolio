About Me

Hello! I’m a third-year B.Tech student at the Central University of Kashmir, driven by a deep interest in Cybersecurity,Networking and Front-End Development. I’m fascinated by how technology connects creativity with security, and I aim to build solutions that are both visually engaging and technically resilient.

My academic journey has helped me develop strong problem-solving skills through languages like C and Java, along with a solid understanding of DBMS and Data Structures & Algorithms. On the analytical side, I enjoy working with Python and its libraries—Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib—to make sense of data and visualize insights effectively.

On the creative front, I’m passionate about front-end development, crafting web interfaces that focus on usability, accessibility, and performance. I’m always exploring new tools, frameworks, and design principles to stay aligned with the evolving tech landscape.
